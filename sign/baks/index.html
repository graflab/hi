How This Demo Works

This demo allows you to securely sign documents by generating a unique Violet ID based on the document's content and a nickname. Once created, the Violet ID is recorded on the blockchain. You can download the transaction receipt (.vgti file) to verify later. No files are uploaded to our servers/transferred over the internet. ID generation happens in your browser on your own computer.
